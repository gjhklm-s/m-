Scoring Rules

>
Each player in the list is assigned to a particular number of points.
Points range from 6-10 based on the player's performance.
If you take the players not in the list ,You will get 4 points only.
Player names should be written correctly or you will not receive the exact points.
To avoid errors, copy and paste the player's name from the list.
The teams with the highest points will be declared the winner.
